About Me

Ali is a seasoned Engineer turned Agile Delivery Leader, boasting over a decade of experience in coaching diverse teams towards realizing their full potential. His approach to Agility is rooted in meeting clients where they are, valuing their insights, and leveraging their inherent wisdom along their evolutionary journey.

With a focus on empowerment through partnership, Ali collaborates closely with his clients to co-create evolutionary journeys that drive lasting change. Drawing from his robust technical background, he excels at distilling complexity and fostering shared understanding among teams.

In 2018, Ali established Elevated Agility, a consultancy dedicated to catalyzing evolutionary change across a spectrum of organizations, including large enterprises, startups, and small businesses. His client portfolio spans diverse domains such as Digital Investments, Web Media Streaming, Trucking Logistics Tracking Management, Identity Access & Management, Personal & Commercial Banking, Global High Value Payments, Infrastructure, and Operations.

As an Accredited Kanban Consultant, Ali has a track record of success in applying the Kanban Method, STATIK, and the Kanban Maturity Model to facilitate systemic evolution and deliver superior outcomes. With a passion for driving continuous improvement and organizational excellence, Ali is committed to guiding teams towards sustainable growth and success.
